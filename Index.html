Turn Blight Into Opportunity. Get Rewarded.

At ReviveHub, we believe the eyes and voices of the community are the key to transformation. That’s why we reward everyday people for spotting and submitting blighted properties in their neighborhood.


  • 1. Earn While You Empower
    Snap a photo. Upload it. If your lead turns into a real estate deal, you earn a cash reward or profit share through our exclusive Joint Venture Program. No license required—just your local knowledge.

  • 2. Watch Your Neighborhood Come Alive
    Track the transformation of every property you submit. From boarded-up eyesore to revitalized home—you’ll see the impact of your action in real-time.

  • 3. Rise on the Leaderboard
    Top contributors get public recognition, digital badges, and early access to new features. Your hustle won’t go unnoticed.

  • 4. Community Giveback
    A portion of profits from every successful project is reinvested into local initiatives—like youth programs, cleanups, and beautification projects—based on feedback from YOU.

  • 5. Pathways to Ownership
    Looking to get deeper? Contributors may be invited to co-invest, co-own, or partner on select redevelopment projects they helped spark.
