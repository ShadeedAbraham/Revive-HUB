Community Incentive Model

At ReviveHub, we empower everyday citizens to become agents of change in their neighborhoods. When a user uploads a photo and details of a blighted or abandoned property, they’re not just reporting a problem—they’re unlocking a potential opportunity.

  • 1. Profit-Sharing Opportunities – Every valid property submission becomes eligible for a Joint Venture Reward. If the property turns into a wholesale deal or redevelopment project, the person who submitted it can earn a finder’s fee or a share of the profit.

  • 2. Trackable Impact – Users can follow the progress of properties they’ve submitted—seeing them go from blight to beauty, and feeling real pride in their neighborhood’s transformation.

  • 3. Gamified Leaderboard & Recognition – Top contributors are recognized on community leaderboards, featured in newsletters, and offered bonus incentives for continued involvement.

  • 4. Community Reinvestment – A percentage of profits from successful deals are reinvested into local beautification projects, youth programs, and community gardens—with input from the top contributing users.

  • 5. Ownership Pathways – In some cases, contributors may be offered a path to co-invest or even take ownership stakes in redevelopment projects sparked by their submission.
