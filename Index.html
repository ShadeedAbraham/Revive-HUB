At ReviveHub, we believe the eyes and voices of the community are the key to transformation. That’s why we reward everyday people for spotting and submitting blighted properties in their neighborhood.

How It Works

Step 1: Snap a Photo

Spot a vacant, abandoned, or blighted property in your area? Take a clear photo showing its condition. You're not just capturing an image — you're helping spark a transformation.

Step 2: Enter the Address

Type in the street address or closest cross streets. Even an approximate location helps us identify the property and run our initial analysis.

Step 3: We Redevelop

Once received, we research ownership, zoning, and value — then explore options like wholesale, fix-and-flip, green space conversion, or community use. You helped light the spark.

Step 4: Track Results

Stay connected as we revive what was lost. Watch the evolution, share in the success, and take pride in your role in reshaping your community — one property at a time.
