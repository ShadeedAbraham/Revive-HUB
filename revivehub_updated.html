How It Works

1. Snap a Photo

Capture a clear image of the blighted property. Make sure to include identifying features.

2. Provide Details

Enter the property's address and a brief description of its condition.

3. Submit Securely

Your information is sent to our team for review and potential action.

Community Incentive Model

At ReviveHub, we believe that community involvement should be rewarded. Our unique incentive model offers tangible benefits for your contributions, fostering a cycle of positive change and shared prosperity.

Profit-Sharing Opportunities

When a property you identify is successfully redeveloped and sold, you may be eligible for a percentage of the profits. It's our way of saying thank you for your valuable contribution.

Local Business Vouchers

Receive vouchers and discounts from local businesses partnering with ReviveHub. Support your local economy while making a difference.

Community Project Funding

A portion of the proceeds from each successful redevelopment project is allocated to a community fund. As a contributor, you get a vote on how these funds are used to further improve your neighborhood.
